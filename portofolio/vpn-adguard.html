VPN + AdGuard

Self-hosted WireGuard with AdGuard to reduce ads and tracking for all connected devices.

Goal

  • Private, stable VPN across phone and PC.
  • Network-level filtering for ads and trackers.

Approach

  • Provision VPS and secure baseline (firewall, updates).
  • Install WireGuard (wg-easy) and AdGuard.
  • Create profiles, test throughput & latency.

Result

  • Reliable connection on mobile and desktop.
  • Noticeably fewer ads and trackers.
